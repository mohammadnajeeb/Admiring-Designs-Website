My name is Minhajur Rahman Khan and I am a young aspiring visual artist born and raised in Aligarh, India. Creative expression is a big part of my life. I am inspired by nature, its design, symmetry and color which is reflected in my current body of work from a signature Mandala designs, to custom graphics & paintings. Currently I'm a fine arts undergrad student at Aligarh Muslim University.
